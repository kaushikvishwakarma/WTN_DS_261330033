# Introduction to AI and ML - Interview Questions

1. **What is Artificial Intelligence?**
   *Answer*: Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating intelligent machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as reasoning, learning, and autonomous decision-making.

2. **What is Machine Learning?**
   *Answer*: Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that provides systems the ability to automatically learn and improve from experience (data) without being explicitly programmed.

3. **Difference between Rule based AI and ML based AI**
   *Answer*: Rule-based AI operates on a strict set of pre-defined "if-then" rules programmed by humans. ML-based AI learns patterns from data autonomously and creates its own models or rules to make decisions.

4. **Difference between Supervised Machine Learning and Unsupervised Machine Learning.**
   *Answer*: Supervised ML uses labeled data (inputs mapped to known outputs) to train models to predict outcomes. Unsupervised ML uses unlabeled data to discover hidden structures, patterns, or groupings (like clustering) without predefined outputs.

5. **Difference between Strong AI and Weak AI.**
   *Answer*: Weak AI (or Narrow AI) is designed to perform one specific task (e.g., virtual assistants, recommendation engines). Strong AI (Artificial General Intelligence) refers to a theoretical machine that possesses general human-level cognitive abilities and consciousness across a wide range of tasks.

6. **What are applications of AI?**
   *Answer*: Applications of AI include natural language processing (chatbots, translation), computer vision (facial recognition, medical imaging), autonomous vehicles, recommendation systems (Netflix, Amazon), and fraud detection in finance.
